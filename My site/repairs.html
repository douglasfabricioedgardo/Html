REPAIRS

At Telcom Hn we have the following technical service:

Screen Replacement:

A damaged screen is one of the most common problems encountered by smartphone users. A screen replacement involves removing the damaged screen and replacing it with a new one.

Battery Replacement:

Over time, a smartphone battery can start to degrade, leading to poor performance and reduced battery life. A battery replacement involves removing the old battery and installing a new one.

Charging Port Repair:

A damaged charging port can prevent a smartphone from charging properly. A charging port repair involves replacing the damaged port with a new one.

Water Damage Repair:

Accidentally dropping a smartphone in water can cause significant damage. A water damage repair involves cleaning and drying the phone and replacing any damaged components.

Camera Repair:

A malfunctioning camera can prevent a smartphone from taking pictures or recording videos. A camera repair involves fixing or replacing the faulty camera module.

Home Button Repair:

A damaged home button can make it difficult to navigate a smartphone. A home button repair involves fixing or replacing the faulty button.

Speaker Repair:

A broken or malfunctioning speaker can affect a smartphone's audio quality. A speaker repair involves fixing or replacing the faulty speaker

Software Repair:

Software issues can cause a smartphone to freeze or crash. A software repair involves diagnosing and fixing any issues with the phone's operating system or software.
